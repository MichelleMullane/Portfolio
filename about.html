Hi I am Michelle Mullane

Full-stack Engineer, based in the eastern United States

My experiences as an Army officer and geological scientist took me all over the world, exploring global landscapes and building relationships with professionals from all backgrounds. My introduction to computer programming was through my graduate research on the Yellow River in China, for which I learned MatLab, Python, and R to compile and analyze millions of data points from disparate technological instrumentation to understand a complex natural system. Most recently, I explored technical solutions to water resource management for a local government combating a decades-long drought. Throughout my varied career, I've found I enjoyed building tools to enable my teams to do their best work - such as building a data visualization dashboard from the ground-up that would later serve as a model for the larger organization. I have spent the past year hyper-focused on immersing myself in advanced JavaScript, React, HTML, CSS, and other computing languages and technologies to prepare for a career in software engineering. I look forward to joining a team where I can continue working on tools and strategies to build a better world.
