Hi I am Michelle Mullane

Full-stack Engineer, based in the eastern United States

My experiences as an Army officer and geological scientist took me all over the world, exploring global landscapes and building relationships with professionals from all backgrounds. My introduction to computer programming was through my graduate research on the Yellow River in China, for which I learned MatLab, Python, and R to compile and analyze millions of data points from disparate technological instrumentation to understand a complex natural system. After graduating from Fullstack Academy's intensive web development certificate program focused on full-stack JavaScript, I joined Black Knight as a fullstack engineering intern where I leveraged Typescript/React and Java/SpringBoot to build multiple features for a revolutionary mortgage servicing technology application. I was promoted to a full-time engineering after three months and immediately began contributing code as a member of an Agile engineer team. ICE recently acquired Black Knight, where I continue to build mortgage servicing technology applications.
